# MatchDay AI: 2-Minute Demo Script ⏱️

**Preparation**: Ensure `DEMO_MODE=true` in the backend `.env` file before starting. Open two separate windows: **Window 1 (Fan View)** and **Window 2 (Organizer View)**.

### Step 1: Select Match (0:00 - 0:15)
- **Action**: In Window 1, select the *Argentina vs Brazil* match at *MetLife Stadium*.
- **Speak**: "Organizing 80,000 fans across multiple languages is chaotic. Let's look at the Fan Experience."

### Step 2: Fan Dashboard & Voice Translation (0:15 - 0:45)
- **Action**: Set language to *Spanish*. Click the mic and ask for a bathroom route.
- **Speak**: "MatchDay AI breaks language barriers. A Spanish fan can ask for directions in their native tongue, and our AI internally routes, processes via RAG, and responds fluently in Spanish."

### Step 3: Increase Crowd (0:45 - 1:15)
- **Action**: Switch to Window 2 (Organizer). Show the Heatmap.
- **Speak**: "Behind the scenes, live sensors feed our WebSocket engine. Notice the North Gate is currently at 95% capacity—a massive red flag for congestion."

### Step 4: Organizer Recommendation (1:15 - 1:40)
- **Action**: Highlight the *AI Decision Support* panel.
- **Speak**: "Instantly, Gemini analyzes the congestion against FIFA safety protocols and generates a real-time recommendation: 'Open Gate 5'."

### Step 5: Broadcast (1:40 - 2:00)
- **Action**: In Window 2, navigate to Broadcast, type "Avoid North Gate", and click Dispatch. Immediately point back to Window 1.
- **Speak**: "The organizer acts. And instantly—via WebSockets, without a page refresh—the fan receives the alert. This is real-time crowd intelligence."
