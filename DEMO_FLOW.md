# 2-Minute Killer Demo Flow ⏱️

Follow this script exactly during your pitch to guarantee maximum impact.

## Preparation
- Open two browser windows side-by-side.
- **Window 1 (Left):** Logged in as `user1@example.com` (Fan), viewing the Fan Dashboard.
- **Window 2 (Right):** Logged in as `org@example.com` (Organizer), viewing the Command Center.

## The Script

### 1. The Fan Problem (0:00 - 0:30)
*("Organizing 80,000 fans across multiple languages is chaos. Watch how we solve it.")*
- In **Window 1**, change the language dropdown to **Spanish**.
- Click the **Mic** button.
- *Say:* "¿Dónde está la zona de comida?"
- *Show:* The AI processes it, translates it, answers in Spanish, and dynamically lights up the **Smart Map**.

### 2. The Organizer Problem (0:30 - 1:15)
*("But what happens when things go wrong? Let's look at the Organizer Command Center.")*
- Switch focus to **Window 2**.
- Show the **Live Heatmap**. Point out that the `North Gate` is currently at **90% capacity (Red/Congested)**.
- Point to the **AI Decision Support** pane.
- *Show:* The AI has automatically generated a real-time recommendation: *"Open East Gate immediately to relieve Gate 5 congestion."*

### 3. The Resolution & Real-Time Alert (1:15 - 2:00)
*("The AI tells the organizer exactly what to do. Now the organizer takes action.")*
- In **Window 2**, go to the **Broadcast Center**.
- Type: *"North Gate is heavily congested. Please use the East Entrance for faster entry."*
- Click **Dispatch Broadcast**.
- *Immediately* point to **Window 1 (Fan View)**.
- *Show:* Without refreshing the page, a massive Red Alert slides onto the Fan's screen instantly.

## The Close
*("Within 2 minutes, MatchDay AI handled multilingual routing, detected a dangerous crowd surge, provided AI-driven operational advice, and evacuated the area using real-time WebSockets.")*
